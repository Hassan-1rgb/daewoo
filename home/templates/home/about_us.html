About Us

Daewoo Express is the first and the biggest foreign investment company with an advanced and organized transport system in Pakistan. It is the only transport company to have a nationwide network with various international trade links.

Being a transport service provider, the company owns a large fleet of buses, technically advanced workshops to maintain the fleet for passenger safety, state-of-the-art terminals, and training institutes for drivers and mechanical staff.

Daewoo Express has been rendering transport facilities since its inception in 1997 to the people of Pakistan.

It is appreciated by the masses as well as by the Governments of Pakistan and Korea for its consistent success. Media of both countries often express appreciation for the revolutionary accomplishment of the company in the transport sector, through various channels.

Daewoo Express has been providing world-class services since 1997, earning trust, recognition, and respect from millions of passengers across Pakistan.
