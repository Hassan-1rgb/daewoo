Daewoo Express News & Updates

Daewoo Express launches Lahore DHA bus service

01 Dec 2015

Daewoo Express inaugurated its international standard urban bus service at DHA Lahore. The new buses come equipped with Euro-3 engines, offering 75% fewer emissions and improved comfort for passengers...

The inauguration was held at the new DHA terminal on Bedian Road. Brig. Zafar Yasin Babar praised the efforts of Daewoo and DHA in providing modern transport solutions.

Daewoo has been operating the Lahore City Bus Service for 17 years, introducing air-conditioned diesel buses for the first time in Pakistan.

Daewoo Express, Growing Bigtime

29 Sep 2015

Since its inception in 1997, Daewoo Express has expanded to over 60 stations nationwide, serving more than 500,000 passengers monthly across Punjab, Sindh, and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa...

In 2015 alone, services expanded to Bahawalnagar, Khanpur, Nawabshah, Khairpur, and Arifwala. Major terminal upgrades were also carried out at DG Khan, Sahiwal, and Jhang.

Partnership with DHA Lahore

01 Oct 2015

Daewoo Express and DHA Lahore launched a landmark project, including an inter-city express service, a DHA local bus service, and a state-of-the-art terminal with shopping facilities in Phase-5...

This project aims to deliver world-class transport services to DHA residents and surrounding communities, marking a new era in urban mobility.

Strategic Alliance with Skywell Automobiles

26 Aug 2020

Daewoo Express and Skywell Automobiles, China, signed a landmark agreement to introduce electric vehicles in Pakistan, with a two-phase plan for bus deployment and local manufacturing...

The agreement was signed simultaneously in Islamabad and China, with federal ministers, senators, and ambassadors in attendance. Phase 1 brings electric buses; Phase 2 will establish a manufacturing plant in Pakistan.

The partnership is expected to bring USD 500 million in foreign direct investment, driving sustainable transport solutions and economic growth.
