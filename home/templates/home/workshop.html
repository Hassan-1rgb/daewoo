Workshops

Objective

"Provide road worthy fleet to operations for the provision of reliable services to commuter"

Driver's Recruiting & Training System: We are recruiting our workers (drivers, technicians, etc) through a comprehensive testing and observation process. During testing, our experts observe the candidates both practically and theoretically.

Pre Selection Training: After initial selection, candidates are given 15 days practical and theoretical training (on/off the field) for final selection. Successful candidates in final tests/medical may become employees of the company.

Monitoring System

Through our highly advanced techograph system we observe the driver's driving on the routes on every arrival of the bus at its destination. Drivers are bound to drive as per rules and regulations (speed limit, stopovers, etc). Violators are recommended for refresher courses or penalized based on severity.

Refresher Trainings

Management arranges refresher courses to overcome negligence observed in workers during duties.

Vehicle Inspection / Fitness System

To maintain roadworthy buses, technical teams monitor 18 site workshops & 4 mobile workshops fully equipped with latest tools. Maintenance follows manufacturer rules and categorized maintenance types.

General Maintenance

Pre-departure and after-arrival inspections include Pit Inspection & Wash Service.

Pit Inspection

Foreman checks engine, oil, water, brakes, tyres, A/V system and rectifies defects noted on crew comment papers.

Wash Service

Buses are washed and cleaned thoroughly inside and out before departure.

Periodic & Preventive Maintenance

Regular replacement of engine oil, filters, steering oil, brake lining, A/C, radiator coolant, and passenger facilities. Quarterly docking service checks engine, chassis, interior, and exterior. Genuine parts are used to ensure safety and comfort.
