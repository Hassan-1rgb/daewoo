Gender Equality Policy

Daewoo is committed to promoting equality, diversity, and inclusion at every level of the organization. We believe in building a workplace where everyone has equal opportunities to succeed, regardless of gender.

  • 1. Ensure equal employment opportunities for all genders.
  • 2. Promote a workplace free from discrimination and harassment.
  • 3. Support women in leadership and decision-making roles.
  • 4. Provide equal pay for equal work regardless of gender.
  • 5. Encourage gender balance in recruitment and promotions.
  • 6. Offer training programs to challenge gender stereotypes.
  • 7. Provide maternity, paternity, and parental leave benefits.
  • 8. Establish policies to prevent workplace harassment.
  • 9. Create mentorship programs for women employees.
  • 10. Ensure flexible working options to support work-life balance.
  • 11. Conduct regular gender equality awareness sessions.
  • 12. Encourage reporting of discrimination without fear of reprisal.
  • 13. Promote safe and inclusive transport services for women passengers.
  • 14. Monitor and publish gender equality performance indicators.
  • 15. Continuously improve policies to close gender gaps.
